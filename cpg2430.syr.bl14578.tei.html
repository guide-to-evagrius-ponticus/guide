Syriac S1 translation of Praktikos
cpg2430-syr-S1
This translation was made sometime before the early 5th century. It lacks the prefatory letter to Anatolios, and was combined with the Gnostikos (version S1, whose chapters are numbered sequentially). The translation is charaterized at times by a literate precision, at other times by studied alteration. This translator might be the same as the translator of version S1 of the Kephalaia Gnostika (CPG 2432).
