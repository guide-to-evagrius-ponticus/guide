Capita gnostica
capita gnostica
Third part of the trilogy (see Praktikos), comprising 540 chapters (90 chapters in six "centuries") discussing the highest levels of contemplation. This text is frequently considered to be behind the condemnations of Origen in 553.
Because of how little survives in Greek, the Syriac translations are key, especially the so-called unexpurgated version (frequently called S2). It is unclear whether the sole Arabic MS (Coptic Patriarchate MS Theol. 152, fol. 147r–177v) is taken from the Greek or Syriac and if the latter, which version.
