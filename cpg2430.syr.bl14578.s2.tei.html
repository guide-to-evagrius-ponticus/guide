First of a trilogy (along with Gnostikos and the Kephalaia gnostika, below) considered one of Evagrius's key works. This treatise contains 100 chapters discussing preliminary discipline of the ascetic life, namely fighting the passions.

Syriac S2 translation of Praktikos
cpg2430-syr-S2
Someone intent on making the text accessible to a broad audience translated version S2 of the Praktikos. The translation tends to be loose, in service to clearer Syriac syntax and semantics, and employs euphemisms or glosses for the sake of comprehension. There is no evidence that he used any other translation. This version differs remarkably from S1 in that it retains the prefatory letter to Anatolios.
